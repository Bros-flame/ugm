About Us

BACKGROUND HISTORY

The organization was formed in 2016 and formally registered in 2019. Over the years the world bank, Stats SA QFL 20Q1 and the International labor organization have shown a significant increase in unemployment in South Africa and putting the country in the first position in the world. The group of aspiring young graduates came together and initiated a movement that seeks solutions and opportunities for unemployed graduates.

OUR OBJECTIVE

To be an NGO, NPO that works hard to finding economic solutions to reduce the level of unemployment in South Africa. The vision is an expression of commitment to:

  • Grow as an NGO
  • Be a sustainable society/organization free of sexism, racism, and grants opportunities to deserving individuals or groups.
